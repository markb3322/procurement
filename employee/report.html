Comprehensive Reporting Features Coming Soon

Once completed, this module will provide you with a full suite of reporting tools including monthly procurement summaries, category-based cost analysis, supplier performance tracking, bid status monitoring, and grand total computations that account for both direct pricing and item-level costs. You will be able to export professional, print-ready reports in multiple formats including Microsoft Word with landscape orientation, ensuring your documentation meets the highest standards for government procurement compliance and audit requirements. Every report will be filterable by date range, category, payment mode, and bid status.
