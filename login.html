Acceptable Use Policy
Procurement Data System & Career Development Support Program
Data & Procurement Policy Effective: January 1, 2026
1Authorized Use & Access

The BEPO-PESO system is a restricted platform for authorized personnel of the Procurement Data System and the Career Development Support Program (CDSP). Access is granted solely to registered members with valid credentials issued by the System Administrator. Each user is personally responsible for maintaining the confidentiality of their login credentials. Sharing, lending, or transferring account access to any other individual is strictly prohibited. Any unauthorized access or breach will be subject to administrative and legal action under RA 10175 (Cybercrime Prevention Act of 2012).

2Procurement & CDSP Data Integrity

All procurement records, member data, career development profiles, and CDSP program information stored within this system are classified as confidential organizational assets. Users must handle such data with the highest standard of care. It is forbidden to copy, export, screenshot, or share any data with unauthorized third parties without prior written approval from the BEPO-PESO Data Officer. All actions performed within this system are logged and subject to regular audit review.

3Data Privacy, Compliance & Member Rights

BEPO-PESO is committed to protecting personal information in full compliance with RA 10173 (Data Privacy Act of 2012). Personal data collected — including names, ID numbers, birthdates, addresses, contact information, and CDSP career records — is used exclusively for operational purposes and shall not be sold, rented, or disclosed without explicit consent. Members have the right to access, correct, and file complaints regarding their data. By logging in, you acknowledge that you have read, understood, and agreed to abide by this Acceptable Use Policy in its entirety.
